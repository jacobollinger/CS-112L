Problem Convert some American units to metric.

See exercise 1-2 of the pdf version of the text.
a) Design a solution to convert miles to kilometers.
b) Design a general solution to convert kilometers per minutes and seconds
to miles per hour.
c) Design a solution to convert miles per hour to kilometers per minute.
d) Design a solution to convert pounds to kilograms.
e) Design a solution to convert degrees Fahrenheit to degrees Celsius.

Each part requires at least one complete sentence of design and a formula.
The design explains the development of the formula.
